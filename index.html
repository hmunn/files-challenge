Judge Dredd

Judge Dredd is the law, merciless and unbending. When his name is spoken by creeps, it is done with fear. He is relentless and will ensure that justice is met out with strict adherence to the law. Nothing can stop him, even though many have tried.

Judge Dredd has appeared in many things since 1977. He originally appeared in issue 2 of 2000 AD. The image above is from the 2012 film Dredd.
